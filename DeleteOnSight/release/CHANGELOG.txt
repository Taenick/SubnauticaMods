Delete On Sight - Changelog
============================

v1.1.0
  - Resource refunds for base pieces and constructable items (seats, lockers, etc.)
  - Storage containers drop their contents before being deleted
  - Items go to inventory first; excess drops ~3m in front of you
  - Materials are added to inventory, or dropped in the world if inventory is full

v1.0.0 
  - Initial release
  - Single target delete (DELETE key)
  - Area delete for colliderless objects (ALT+DELETE)
  - Console command (delete)
  - Base piece force-removal with geometry rebuild
  - Auto-cleanup of wall-mounted items in deleted rooms
  - Vehicle deletion (Seamoth, Prawn Suit, Cyclops)
  - Creature and world object deletion
  - Terrain and player protection
  - Configurable keybind and area radius
  - Save persistence for all deletion types
